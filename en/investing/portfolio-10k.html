BRACE — BriefRooms Adaptive Conviction Engine

BRACE is the BriefRooms Adaptive Conviction Engine. It combines business quality, results and revisions, valuation, market confirmation, risk, context and an evidence ledger.

The model learns through a controlled loop: decision → outcome → success or error analysis → tested improvement → only then a rule change. Decision history is never rewritten, and every modification must pass a backtest and the champion–challenger gate. BRACE does not self-modify pillar weights or decision thresholds and never executes trades.
