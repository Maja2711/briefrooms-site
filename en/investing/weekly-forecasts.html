Weekly market scenarios

EUR/USD and S&P 500 futures — method, weekly log and later review.

Setup

A scenario is created on Sunday for the coming week. The starting price is recorded on Monday morning and the final price after the Friday weekly close.

The method is stored here: methodology.json. Change history: method_changelog.md.
