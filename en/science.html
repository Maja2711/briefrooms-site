Li-ion batteries in a nutshell

Charging/discharging = lithium ions shuttling between anode and cathode.

Three essentials

  1. Energy density enables lighter phones and EVs.
  2. Aging is faster with heat, 0–100% cycles, frequent fast-charging.
  3. Safety relies on BMS guarding temps/voltages/currents.

Practice: daily charge to ~80–90%, avoid heat (>40°C), don’t store at 0%.
