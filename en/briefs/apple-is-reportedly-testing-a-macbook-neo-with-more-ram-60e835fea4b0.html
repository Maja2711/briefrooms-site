Apple is reportedly testing a MacBook Neo with more RAM

Core point

Apple is reportedly developing an updated version of the MacBook Neo with a new A19 Pro processor and increased RAM. The new model may feature 12GB of RAM, an upgrade from the original 8GB. There are also plans to refresh the MacBook Neo with new colors. Additionally, Apple is expected to release new iMacs with OLED screens and other long-rumored updates like the M6 MacBook Pro.
