Black Sea: a strategic choke point

Trade lanes, energy routes and military presence make it a volatility hotspot.

Key takeaways

  • Trade: grain & bulk flows via Bosporus into the Med.
  • Energy: pipelines and LNG terminals raise Europe’s stakes.
  • Security: fleets & restricted zones → recurring incidents.

Operationally: diversify routes/insurance; expect price swings when risk rises.
