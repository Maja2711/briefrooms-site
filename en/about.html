About BriefRooms

A short explanation of how BriefRooms works, which sources we rely on, and what you can use it for.

BriefRooms was created for people who want to stay informed but do not have time to read dozens of long articles every day. Instead of flooding you with information, we pick relevant topics and turn them into short, practical summaries.

The platform is organised into five rooms: News, Geopolitics, Health, Science and Investments. In each room you will find selected materials from reliable sources, written in clear language and focused on key claims, main takeaways and potential consequences.

Sources, research and evidence

Our goal is to rely on trusted sources and research. We try to separate facts, data, commentary and hypotheses, and to highlight areas where knowledge is limited.

AI in the background, humans in control

In the background we use AI to search sources and prepare first drafts of briefs. Summaries should be reviewed and edited to keep them concise, understandable and as unbiased as possible.

How should you use BriefRooms?

BriefRooms is not a traditional news portal and does not provide financial or medical advice. Treat it as a starting point: a quick overview that helps you decide what is worth exploring further in original sources.
